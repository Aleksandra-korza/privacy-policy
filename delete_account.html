Delete Your Account – Klub Książki

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, you can request it by email.

How to request account deletion

Send an email to book.club.poland@gmail.com
with the subject line: Delete my account.

In the message, please include the email address you use to log in to the app (OTP login).

What data will be deleted

The following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address
  • Your account and profile information
  • Your created book rooms / reading rooms
  • Any other data directly associated with your account

Data that may be temporarily retained

For security and fraud-prevention purposes, minimal technical logs may be kept for up to 30 days before being permanently deleted.
